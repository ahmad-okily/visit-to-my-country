Premium Essential Ingredients: Macarons are made from almond flour and ground sugar, giving it a distinctive taste and rich flavor

Double texture: consists of two thin and fragile layers with a delicious creamy filling in between, creating a contrast between soft and crispy

Variety of flavors: Macarons can be prepared in multiple flavors such as vanilla, chocolate, pistachio, berries, and lemon,allowing for a diverse experience.

French origins: Croissants are one of the most prominentFrench desserts, and their origins go back to Austria, but they have become a symbol of French pastries after becoming part of French cuisine.

Varied formations: Croissants can be prepared in different flavors, such as chocolate croissants or croissants stuffed with cheese or meat, making them suitable for all tastes.

Meticulous preparation: Preparing croissants requires special techniques such as fermentation and rolling to create fine layers, which makes their preparation time-consuming and effort-intensive.

Variety of meat dishes: French cuisine offers a variety of meat dishes such as boeuf bourguignon (beef cooked in red wine), côte de puy (grilled lamb chops) and steak frites (steak with French fries), which combine.

Focus on quality: In French cuisine, choosing fresh meat is essential to achieving distinctive flavours. Poultry such as duck and chicken are used, in addition to beef and lamb, which are carefully prepared in various dishes.

Distinctive French sauces: Sauces are added to meat dishes to enhance the flavour. The most famous French sauces are “Sauce Béarnaise” .
