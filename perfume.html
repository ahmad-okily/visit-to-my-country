PERFUME

Chanel No. 5: Known a timeless classic, this floral-aldehyde fragrance combines rose, jasmine, and citrus for an elegant, powdery aroma. It embodies sophistication and has
been a symbol of refined femininity since its release in 1921.

Dior Sauvage: A fresh, spicy fragrance with notes of bergamot, Sichuan pepper, and amber, giving it a raw, bold character. It's celebrated for its rugged masculinity, appealing to those who enjoy a modern yet classic scent.

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium: This warm, sweet scent combines coffee, vanilla, and white flowers for an_ intense, seductive appeal. It's perfect for night wear and evokes a sense of confidence and mystery.

Tom Ford Black Orchid: A luxurious, exotic fragrance with deep notes of black truffle,plum,andpatchouli, making it dark and opulent. It's favored by those who appreciate a rich, complex scent that stands out.

Creed Aventus: A fresh, fruity fragrance with pineapple, birch, and musk notes, offering a strong yet versatile scent. It's popular for its refined, powerful aroma that leaves a lasting impression..
